Game info:

D - days

N - number of companies

K - shares

R - Fee


1. Can't have more than K shares from a company  

2. Can only perform one transaction per day ( buy or sell as many shares as he wants from that company ) 

3. For every share bought, john charges a fee. P.E. if john buys 2 shares in a transaction the exchanging fee would be 2 x R
   There is no exchanging fee in selling.Â´


| INPUT |

1st line - Gives 1,2 or 3 to choose which task to do

2nd line - Gives values of N , D , K , R

following N lines - Give the values of shares separated by days, 
    Example: Line 1: 2 5 3 10 (meaning that in day 1, the value of the shares is 2, in day 2 the value of the shares is 3... etc)
